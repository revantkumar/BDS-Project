This proposal is integral to not only sustaining the innovative, successful pace of the U.S. tech industry, but also keeping talent within this nation. U.S. university invest endless resources in hardworking and intelligent international STEM students. It is important the government does its best to make sure those investments provide returns to the economy. The longer STEM students are allowed to stay in America, the more opportunities they have to obtain the H1-B visa and remain in America, working at American companies helping them grow and succeed.
