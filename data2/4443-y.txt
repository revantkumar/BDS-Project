Liu Cao The extension for F-1 students with STEM degrees does not necessarily become a "threat" to the US society. According to the survey conducted by the Education Department, the current demand in STEM-related job positions largely exceeds the supply, along with a predicted 14% to 62% increase in the next five years. Therefore, an extension in OPT of such fields can highly resolve this issue and help the US economy and industry revive and prosperous.
