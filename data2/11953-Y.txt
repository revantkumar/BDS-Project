With the increasing competition in job market most immigrant graduate students may need few months after graduation to find a OPT job. Most companies ask for minimum 1 year of US experience to sponsor work visa. By increasing OPT extension from 17 months to 24 months students will have better opportunity to gain enough professional experience and be able pursue a career in the academic area they received training. It attracts more international students to pursue graduate studies in the U.S and lawfully remain in US until they acquired desired skills.
