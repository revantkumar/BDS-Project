The extension of OPT not only benefits universities in the US but also has positive influence on companies in the US. First, with a longer OPT, students all around the world are more eager to study further in the US rather than other countries. Thus, universities in America may have more fundings to do more research in science which is good to the scientific development of America. Second, with a longer OPT, students who are working in the companies can be more prepared to broaden the business in other countries. Therefore, those companies will have more opportunities in do business in other countries, which is good to the economy of America. Last, students who can work with other people in America have potential to do creative things in their field of study which will have largely positive impact on the development of the US.
