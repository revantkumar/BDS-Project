Chunhao Shan The new policy about extending the OPT for STEM students is more than the benefits for foreign students. It is also a great opportunity for the US economy.

The US has the best universities and research institutions and the most powerful science and technology in the world. The fundamental of these successful organizations are those innovative and hard-working immigrants around the world. They came the US to get education and use what they learnt to build the US economy. Some of them chose to go back to their home countries after their success in the US and keep a closed economical relationship with the US and even make more investments in the US to create more jobs.
