I think the OPT extension has very positive effects on economy of USA. First, F-1 graduate students with highly competitive knowledge in STEM major will definitely make contributions to keep the technologies staying at state-of-art level and facilitate the development of economy of USA, which in turn increase the job opportunities for Americans and improve the quality of citizens' life. Second, since those students are highly educated, they will absolutely increase the diversities of American culture without any risks of increasing criminal rate.
