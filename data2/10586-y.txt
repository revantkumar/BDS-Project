Akashnil Dutta It is absolutely important to allow STEM extension for OPT because there exists an excess demand for STEM jobs in the United States. These jobs don't harm the american economy at all. Aside from the employers growing their business, the money earned by OPT employees are spent in the US which fuel other businesses and create more jobs.

Because H1B visa is lottery based, lots of international students have to quit their job if there is no extension plan. Hence it is absolutely important for there to be a path from F1 visa to H1B visa that is facilitated by OPT.
