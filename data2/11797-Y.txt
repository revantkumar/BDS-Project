The OPT extension plan is definitely beneficial for those talented aliens in US as well as the country as a whole. First, the extension creates longer window for those who are willing to generate their value within this country, which not only enable them to improve their skills but also create more opportunity for firms to realize and exploit their potentials. Secondly, this policy can encourage those who are not certain of their future residency to stay in US for longer time, potentially increase their exposure to US society, and many of them will be likely to continue working for the general welfare of this nation
