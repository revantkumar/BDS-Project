Ben Su The OPT extension enables a large amount of international students with high technical background and working potential to contribute to American high-tech industry. High-tech industry is developing so fast in other countries around the world, the US cannot afford to lose such a great opportunity to become a leader in this area. Almost every industry, including medical, manufacturing and service industry, can be extensively influenced by the development of technology, to which international students contribute most in the US.
Besides, the US has always been known as a country valuing its diversity. OPT extension fits and improves this country's core value, expanding American's impact in the world.
In conclusion, the OPT extension is beneficial to both American economy and the US's international image.
