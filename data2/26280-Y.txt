The OPT expansion is important to US as well as it is a great opportunity to F-1 students.
It is harmful for US enterprises to close themselves to domestic workers. Hosts of US enterprises take pride in their international competitiveness. This is heavily due to contribution of employees coming from all over the world to help with the company's all-rounded decision making and international market observations. A well developing reaching out to the whole globe will effectively provide new job opportunities for US citizens. The economy of US can stay energetic only by keeping its competitiveness internationally. Accepting F-1 students is the most cost-effective way to achieve such goal, as they come from different culture to be in average better equipped with a cross-cultural view, which will be invaluable to their US employers. Facilitating F-1 students working for US employers is also maintaining US economic prosperity.
