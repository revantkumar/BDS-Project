Shunong Sun Although OPT extension is important to international students, it is more essential to the US as a nation. With extension, it will be easier and more effective for US employers to find the best qualified employees and therefore benefit their businesses. Also, STEM majors in US colleges and Universities are far fewer than other majors due to the rigorousness and difficulty. STEM OPT extension will surely become one of the advantages which encourages more students to choose STEM as a major or as elective classes. This trend towards becoming a STEM major will surely benefit the society as a whole.
