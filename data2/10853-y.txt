Zhengren Zhu OPT option is an important part of a student's post-graduation career development. The current length of the OPT is too short for an effective and valuable training and/or entry-level work experience, especially for STEM students. Moreover, for international students to realize the true value of higher education in the US, an opportunity to receive hands-on practical trainings that apply directly the tools and knowledge that their education has provided is crucial. And real-world experience in the US is without doubt the best way to reach this goal. As a result, the proposed rule of extending the OPT timeframe should be appreciated.
