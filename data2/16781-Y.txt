A nation's competency is highly dependent on the talents that serve it. Also, It is a fundamental Economics theorem that competition would help maximize everyone's well-being. Foreign STEM students brings talents to the US. They also brings competition that would inspire all citizens or non-citizens of the country to work hard and excel. Simply make it hard for these foreign talents to work in the U.S would be short-sighted. 
That being said, I advocate this proposal to extent the OPT period by 24 months.
