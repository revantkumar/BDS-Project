Immigrants, historically, are one of the most vibrant parts of the population and are vitally important for the prosperity of the economy. Within the immigrants, foreign students are the most energetic part. They bring in the best of the world and provide the perspective of the world. They have already well-trained in the university and often with the advanced post-graduate program. They normally becomes the most contributing force in the society. It is necessary to give them the opportunities for employment here to complete their training and to smooth their transition to the work force. Given the current other regulation hurdle, 2 year is necessary for a successful transition. If they are not as good, the 2-year limit can naturally root them out. But it will benefit the society greatly by incorporate them into the American work force and economy if they can succeed.
