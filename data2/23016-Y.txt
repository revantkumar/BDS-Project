This act will provide a win-win solution:

1. It will attract many talented students to work for US companies, which makes these companies more competitive.
2. Native Americans with STEM degrees are not enough for US companies, and international student can fill the gap.
3. US companies prefer native students to international students if native students are capable for that position. Therefore, Americans do not need to worry about international students take their jobs.
4. The act will attract more international students to study in US, which is a huge revenue for US. It will create more jobs for Americans.
