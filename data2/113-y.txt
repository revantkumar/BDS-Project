Ramona Mihailescu STEM has been proved to be a mix of disciplines with a severe lack of skilled professionals. Why would the US take so many steps back from the progress that has been made by STEM ambassadors globally? International STEM students bring that fresh air and inspiration that younger generations need to understand the potential of their development in STEM fields. An extension of 24 months could aid immensely, as international students come with different perspectives, sometimes from countries even poorer in STEM skills. They should be supported, not prevented from giving a hand.
