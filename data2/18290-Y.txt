This rule should be strongly supported for the following reasons:
1. This rule has great benefits to not only US companies but also the whole society. With STEM students working in US companies, the companies gain high-quality works. The products that are built together with STEM students also benefit the society. People's live are improved by the high-tech products.
2. It is good for the US society to have diversity in races. It is the spirit of US that gives everyone equal chance. With this rule approved, the STEM students can have more chances to benefit US society and have more time to practice their skills.
3. With longer OPT, more talented students will be attracted to the US. The development of IT and other areas will be faster and better. 
4. The foreign students are also consumers in the US. Their existence bring benifits to local economy.
