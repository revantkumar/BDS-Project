OPT is a great opportunity for both STEM students and tech companies to apply professional knowledge to industrial practises. STEM students have spented years in the United States studying how to solve real life STEM challenges. In their OPT period, they could contribute to the improving of life qualities and satisfaction in United States by working for a company or non-profit organization.
The extension for OPT is a crucial part of the OPT program. While the original 1 year OPT period is beneficial for STEM students in terms of finding the applications of their knowledge, the extension period really help them to make changes to the existing methods and create new inventions that may shape to world.
