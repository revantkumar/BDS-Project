This policy is definitely favourable to national interests. 
1. It's not wise to give up on the young graduates who have benefited from US education resources but will serve for another country. That will be a huge waste of human resources. 
2. It will introduce new competition to citizen graduates. Although might not be happy at the first place, the citizen graduates will become more competitive and thus benefit from this policy in the long term and in a macro point of view. 
3. It's obvious good news to employers, which means also good news to the entire economy.
