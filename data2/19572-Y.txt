STEM OPT extension for foreign students is not only the most important thing, it but also is the most important for US economy and development of IT. First, based on the theory of Freedom in the U.S, it is American spirit to give everyone equal chance. Talented students who come to U.S. to study deserves more time to practice their skills after graduation. Second, it is good for US economy to have talented foreign people work in science and engineering area. Third, it is good for US education since longer OPT will attract more talented students coming to study in the U.S.
