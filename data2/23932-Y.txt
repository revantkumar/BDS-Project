This regulation will not only offer more opportunities for F-1 students with STEM degrees to work in the United States, but also more importantly, will greatly help to support the development of the US economy. This extension does not squeeze out any job opportunity for US citizens. Some people may be suffering from unemployment currently, but it surely has nothing to do with the OPT regulation. The US universities trained a lot of qualified and competent students to work for US organizations, these students should have the work authorization to contribute what they have learnt to where they receive their education.
