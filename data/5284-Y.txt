This is a very good stage that US starting to give the opportunity to international student who wants to explore their skills and ability. In fact, the first problem that most of international student facing nowadays are not be able to work outside the campus and gain any experience from their major of study. Many of them want to explore their experience and build more connection in US, but unobstructed by the fact they cannot stay longer for more than certain years. By giving more opportunities and training to International students, we can help them to achieve what they need and also build relationship with other countries.
