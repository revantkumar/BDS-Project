Pratik Mrinal With the extension of STEM OPT timelines, the students would be able to better implement their learning in a professional environment thereby making the best usage of their university education, which could result in being worthless if not given the opportunity to do so. The OPT extension is perfectly in accordance to the huge money investment made by these international students giving a full meaning to their education. Also, many of these international students end up contributing heavily in professional and research fields thereby helping not the industry only, but sometimes whole humankind as well. We should think of the potential of these students and hence come together for devising and development of all of us. Hence, OPT extension is an appropriate decision leading to a win-win situation for all the parties involved.
