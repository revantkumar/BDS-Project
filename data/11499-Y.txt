It should be a common sense that attract to highly educated foreigners is a key to the success of united state. With the opt extension, F-1 students are more likely to contribute to the nation, creating more value and improving economy and job market.

On the other hand, F-1 students are usually big consumer in the united states. In university, it is well known that international students need to pay double the fees (at least in california). And students are big consumers in local housing, cloths, food market.
