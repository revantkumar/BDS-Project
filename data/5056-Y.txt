Liwei Zhou One of the foundation of the prosperity of the U.S. is science and technology. During the past decade, incredibly rapid development in science in the U.S. lets the world know the power of this country. However, it was not achieved just by the native. In fact, millions of foreigners came to this land and made their contribution. It was achieved by the world. To bring more talented people to this land, to keep U.S. prosperous, OPT program and its extension play critical roles. The program provides great opportunities to foreigners to make them feel safe to stay here and make their contribution. In short,
this program is an investment of people, an investment of knowledge. And please recall,
"An investment in knowledge pays the best interest." -- Benjamin Franklin
