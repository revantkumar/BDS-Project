The new proposed rule is a good improvement of the previous rule.
The OPT STEM Extension Should be Retained for the Benefit of STEM Graduates and I would also advocate permanent residency for them as the United States needs the best talent graduating from its best schools to take it forward.
Given that the H-1B visa program is a lottery driven one and not a merit based procedure, this rule provides more merit to both the students and the employers. 
Foreign students who graduate with an advanced degree in STEM fields from U.S. universities positively impact the U.S. economy and employment opportunities of native-born workers.
