The improved OPT extension, besides providing immediate benefits to we international students who are currently pursuing post-secondary degrees, will benefit the United States from a big picture view.This new rule allows students to get more working experiences after their graduation, which is crucial for one's career development. I not only want a good education but also great work experiences to make myself stand out in the career field I am pursuing. Therefore, being able to work at the country after graduation is one of the most decision criteria when I am considering studying abroad. Hence, a longer OPT period will certainly increase the competitiveness of educational institutions in the United States and attracts more talent students from around the world.
