This OPT extension is very important to the international students to help US industry providing power and strength on the new technology. With few H1-B visas issued each year, international students have to enter a lottery for the working visa. Most of the international students' work is vital to the US economy and technology. Thus it is very important to extend the STEM OPT period such that more chances could happen when applying for the limited working visa to have the US economy growing.
