Mia Cooper OPT extension is very important to IT companies no matter now or in the future. Currently, a lot of companies are hiring international students who are on their OPT extension status. And the group of these kinds of students is huge. If the extension get invalid, it is extremely hard to find the same candidates to fill in these positions. Especially, these industries are lacking local labors. 

Also, these F-1 students are contributing to United States in multiple ways. As they are from the STEM majors, they are always working on high-tech or research area. These are directly related to the science innovation and research. Thus, it is better to improve and expand the training opportunities for F1 students.
