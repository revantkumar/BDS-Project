OPT extension for F1 STEM student is a crucial and beneficial policy for both the student and the US development. On one hand, the extension policy provide a great opportunity for student to fully understand and apply the acquired knowledge. On the other hand, what's more important is that the well trained STEM student is a strong work force which can boost the US economy and technology development. Given above two points, the proposed rule for STEM OPT extension is a fundamental step for US to ensure the leading position in the world.
