For years Americans trained in technical fields such as engineering and computer technology have struggled to compete in a job market which favors foreign nationals, ostensibly paying them less than qualified Americans, but too often not even offering work to citizens at any pay. Both new graduates and older employees at the peak of their abilities are frequently unable to find appropriate technical employment.
