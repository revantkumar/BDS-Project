The 24-month STEM OPT extension rule will benefit American peoples' daily life. There is a significant percentage of foreign workers in the high-tech companies that you can come up with in 3 seconds: Facebook, Amazon, Microsoft and Intel. These companies seem to be a part of your daily life now. The foreign workers play an important role there, some of which are fresh grads who uses OPT to work. The new OPT extension rule will make him/her work 7 months longer in the company, meaning he/she can contribute their talented work 7 months longer to make your daily life more convenient. Although one OPT worker's contribution is limited, it becomes significant that 10,000 or obviously, even more OPT workers get the opportunities to work longer.
