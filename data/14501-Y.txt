The F1-OPT program is a strong support to both U.S economy and U.S social welfare. These international students created demand and are not the competitors of U.S. workers. They often have over-achieving educational background and maintain special skills which are not common but critical in the U.S.

On the other hand, the H-1B visa applications of the labor outsourcing should be banned. These outsource companies from India send tens of thousands of Indian workers who are much cheaper to the U.S. and squeezed the U.S. working opportunities to overseas.
