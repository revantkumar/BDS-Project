The high-tech companies in U.S. need and must hire the most talented students in STEM areas to keep their leading positions in the global markets. The foreign students, who are mostly from the top universities in their home countries and received educations in U.S, are the most valuable resources. The new rule will help the company to hire and keep these talented and hard-working students, and therefore, is of critical importance to the economy of U.S. 

The new rule will not make the U.S. citizen difficult to land a job. On the contrary, it will contribute to the growth of U.S. economy and create more jobs.
