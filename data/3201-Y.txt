The citizens of the United States who have studied in the STEM fields have been experiencing a long period of depressed wages. A major contributing factor has been a labor oversupply. This is caused by various programs admitting so many STEM graduates from the foreign. I have heard that you intend to expand the number of years available under OPT. I was the officer of a University foreign student organization. I realize that when foreign student programs started the expectation was that the students would acquire skills and return to their original country. Your actions are damaging both the prospects of American students and the development of the sending countries.
