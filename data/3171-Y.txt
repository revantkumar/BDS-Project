If you believe the problem of unemployment can be solved by providing job opportunities based on citizenship, then you are wrong. When do we start to believe that we can solve problems by closing the door? 

Input of talented people can help US products win the global competition and extend the market all over the world. If we push these people out of the door to our competitors, I don't think you will naively believe that they won't be able to develop techniques/products that eventually outperform ours and kick US out of the stage.
