Fully support this new regulation. 

First of all, it will make American college programs more attractive and appealing to foreign students, which may help attract more talents to the US and more innovations will emerge as a result. Innovations are the true power that is propelling the economy of this nation. 

Besides, according to news reports such as http://www.sfchronicle.com/business/article/Tech-leaders-make-immigration-a-moral-issue-6575103.php and http://www.cnn.com/2015/08/14/opinions/yang-google-ceo-immigration/, tech companies can just find sufficient skilled workers without considering foreign students. The extension will allow more F-1 students get the right training and practice to become qualified workers and fill in the gaps of workforce. We just cannot ignore the contributions of tens of thousands foreign workers in the most recent Internet economy booming.
