Frank Jin The United States will be benefit from the OPT Extension program.

Every year, the US government will only issue new H1B working visa up to 85,000. As a result, the OPT extension program will NOT increase the total number of new foreign employees, but only increase the chance of the technical employee to get the working visa.

The OPT Extension program helps to classify the applicants who are urgently needed by the US employment market and who are not. Ultimately, the program helps the US to keep and improve its technological advantage in the world.
