They need to pass a Constitutional Amendment to make English the official language of the U.S., eliminate affirmative action, repeal the 1965 immigration act (going back to the 1921 immigration act), and secure the border before they even consider a guest worker program. It should be modeled after the Bracero program. The guest worker can come to the U.S. to do the job, so long as he or she does not take an American job, and goes back to their country after the job is done.
