Over 40% of engineers in companies like Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Apple, Amazon are international immigrants. They've bene educated in the US and have the best technical skills in the world. They helped the employers generated millions of dollar per person in the industry. Loosing one of them could hurt the company. 

The STEM extension helps to retain more talented students and international workers in the US to help the American internet and technology industry become the best in the world. If we let them go to another country, it might weaken the competitiveness of the US technology industry.
