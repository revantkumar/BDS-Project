In the phase of competition in the global education and leadership, it would benefit the U.S. to implement the STEM OPT. this is the only advantage the U.S. has over many other competitors who are expanding such programs and attracting prospective international student. Most of these students are seeking technological and scientific advancement which is the direction of the globe. Because these fields require more time to truly develop, STEM OPT is necessary in addition to the one year OPT. It will lead to technological advancements, high research output and overall development of the United States and its educational systems.
