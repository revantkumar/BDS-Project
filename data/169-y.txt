Rebecca Parsons Expanding opportunities for STEM graduates benefits employers in high-tech industries by helping them to maintain connections with colleges and increase visibility on campus. STEM-related disciplines are responsible for many of the societal innovations that make our world better. One of our richest sources of employment and economic growth will be jobs that require skills in these areas, collectively known as STEM. This rule will strengthen the relationships between America and other countries. That is, they can share more information, more resources and more opportunities.
