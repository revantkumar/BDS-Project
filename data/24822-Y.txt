The international students who get there advanced degrees usually become a great assets for US companies, who have been contributing much for US companies and thus help with US economy. The government should give international student more time to transfer their knowledge to power of improving US economies. 

Besides, OPT is a key reason that US has attracted many elites from all over the world to US education system. Many international students has became elites in US society such as managers and professors. Cancellation of CPT will prevent many potential elites from pursuing a higher degree in US.

It is true that competition exists in job market, however, the more elite students working in US companies, the more job opportunities will be created because of the positive effect of international students to the growth of US companies. Moreover, some international students even start up their own companies which created a bunch of job positions for US citizens.
