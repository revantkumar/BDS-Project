Melanie Rampling International students are also helping to fill skills shortages at a time when the US share of the world's science and engineering graduates is declining. International students also help promote a global learning environment. If this rule is not passed, foreign STEM grads will take their skills and education to their home countries, costing America the opportunity for job-creating innovation. One of our richest sources of employment and economic growth will be jobs that require skills in these areas, collectively known as STEM.
