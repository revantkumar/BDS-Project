This is a really good plan for both US tech companies and high quality foreign technicians. If students have only 1 year OPT, they might choose to go to companies in other countries since the possibility of getting H-1b visa in US is becoming less and less. And that will hurts the US top level tech companies a lot. The visa status of plenty of their employees will become unlawful which forces talents to leave. So the 24 months OPT extension is really essential at this time point.
