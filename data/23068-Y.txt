The OPT and the extension program is one of the most important reasons to attract foreign students to study in the US, and the F1 student workers are really important to not only the companies and also the US. The F1 students with stem degree contribute a lot to high tech companies. And also, they consumed a lot, like rent a house, pay tuition to school, pay to tax to the government and so on. If the OPT extension program stops, the country will lose a lot of income and a lot of companies will lose a lot of employees suddenly and they can not operations as before.
