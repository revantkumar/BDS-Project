The OPT program, including the extension program, has been playing an important role in completing the higher education system of STEM majors. For STEM major students, it is crucial to have a chance to spend a significant period, more than 1-year, on practice their knowledge in the best institutes and companies. Without this program, the education of US reputation will decline to training mill rather than a source of advanced science and technology for the world, including US itself. The committee should perform comprehensive study on the OPT length before making any significant changes on the existing rules.
