In general, the high-tech job, which involves science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, demands highly skilled master graduates. In todays US market, the highly skilled master graduates, who own the technical background and the spirit of innovation, are critical to the booming of economy and extremely shorted in the amount.

The STEM OPT extension provides the opportunity for both the high-tech company and the skilled master graduates to fulfill their demands. One is seeking skilled worker to drive innovation, one is looking for practical training and valuable working experience. It helps to make US economy strong and competitive, and should be supported by US government.
