Improving and expanding training opportunities for F-1 nonimmigrant students with STEM degrees and CapGap relief for all eligible F-1 students is very important for F-1 international STEM students and the United States. As you know, international STEM students have contributed a lot to the technology progress in the United States. The economy in the United States is keeping increasing, therefore more and more international STEM student with professional skills are needed. Decreasing the number of international STEM students will definitely slow down the economy progress in the United States.
