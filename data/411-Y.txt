Jingyan Tang The additional OPT time would be helpful for STEM graduates as well as American. First of all, nowadays, a lot of potential companies are facing a situation that it is very hard to hire a proper employee, which comes from the fact that the potential employees are way less than the needs of the companies, and STEM graduates are able to provide excellent help for these situation. Besides, for the excellent STEM graduates, the companies often provide them opportunities to work at other countries and then help them transfer to other visa status when OPT expires, which will waste a lot of money and time for the American companies. As a result, the additional OPT time would bring a lot of benefits to the American companies.
