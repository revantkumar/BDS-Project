The OPT extension is crucial for STEM students to find a job and get a legal working visa. Until now, most high tech companies hire students with STEM degree, mostly for their trained skills. In this case, these students can bring their specialized technique into practice. And also, as the prosperity and progress of the United States are highly depended on the diversity and highly-trained immigrants, the OPT STEM extension will definitely help for the social welfare of the country and bring vitality to the economy.
