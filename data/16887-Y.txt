For long time, US success in Technologies and Economy comes from a diverse environment where talents from all over the world gather to innovates and make a changes to the world. People with skills and competence in Engineering and Technologies should be honored and be given more opportunities to improved on their study; These talented students will enhance the current technology and economy of US if they are given the chance and are motivated. Extended OPT attracts more highly qualified international students to come to US school, improving the competitiveness of the school, and at the same time, mitigating the budget problem of the school. Certainly, extended OPT will make a job position more selective, but at the same time, this makes the person who is taking the job more qualified. Therefore in general this improves the qualities of employees in the company who is willing to offers the opportunities to international students.
