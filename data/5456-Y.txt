Ji Li OPT stem extension is important not only for foreign students but also for the US high tech. The STEM jobs require people with high level skills in particular field such as software engineering and hardware design. The majority of US native people seeking jobs won't be affected. However, if opt extension is terminated, companies will lose these foreign students with high level skills and these students will instead work in companies in other countries. In today's world, it won't take much time for a company to lose its leading position in market if it loses the best NGUs.
