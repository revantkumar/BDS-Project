This measure is needed as a remedy to the broken legal immigration system. Students in the STEM who come to study in some of the top universities here in the US can be a great asset to the economy. Forcing them to go back can only be detrimental to the US in the long run. There are already other major cities like Bangalore that are becoming huge startup-hubs and can rival Bay Area in terms of talent and funding. If highly educated masters and PhDs start returning back from US then it is inevitable that the next Google or even Tesla might come up there.
