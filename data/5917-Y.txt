The OPT STEM act is definitely positive to the US economy, especially in the high-tech industries such as Internet related industries and finance. These industries is imperatively demanding employees who have great knowledge in math, science, engineering and other STEM covered fields. A significant portion of these group is composed of international employees who study in the US. If STEM act failed, the US will lose many of these capable and, in other words, useful people. This is an unnecessary lost to everyone here in the US.
