Increasing the STEM OPT extension from existing 17 months to 36 months would be a great initiative as it has got more positive impact on the employer-student perspectives. This extension allows international students to gain enough practical knowledge and help them in establishing themselves in technical positions and contributing towards the development of US economy as a whole. There are also International students with entrepreneurial skills, who are looking to establish their Startups in US economy, which in turn boosts the employment opportunities and increases the economy to a great factor. Extending the OPT will also increases the probability of obtaining H-1B visas, which would help employers who can use the technical skills of students on OPT in a broader way. Considering all the above factors, it would definitely be a win-win scenario for both the international students and employers looking for young talent and in turn helping the US economy in extending its horizons and raising the standards.
