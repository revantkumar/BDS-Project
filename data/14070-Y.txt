I DO SUPPORT the opt-extension. In recent years, high technology industry like IT, electronic and biotechnology has been the chief driving force of United State economic growth. Foreign students have played a critical role in this trend. Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google are two good example of international employees fulfilled themselves in United States. With OPT extension, United Stated could continually attract best talents from all around the world, and remain leader in this ever-changing world. Otherwise, lot of talented new international new graduates will have to seek new opportunities in their home or other countries, which will probably weaken the competitiveness of United States. In all, the opt-extension act will benefits high tech field and increases the whole economic.
