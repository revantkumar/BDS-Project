Lingjing Jiang It is really important for the U.S. to attract highly talented foreigners and provide them the opportunities to contribute to this country. This new act on OPT extension is surely a welcome to foreign talents, and a wise choice for a better future of the United States. Due to the huge shortage of H1B, the extension of OPT to 24 months for STEM can definitely relieve the pressure of foreign employees and their hiring country.
