Lan Wang I support improving and expanding OPT because F-1 students contribute huge amount of time and effort to study and enrich their experiences in the U.S., and they deserve the chance to find a job that matches their capability among the job-hunting world that is becoming more and more competitive. On the other hand, this is also a great opportunity for the U.S. to absorb high-tech talents for STEM fields to make up the openings, and to increase the American economy as a whole to another level.
