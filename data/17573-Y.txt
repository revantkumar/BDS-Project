By improving and expanding training Opportunities for F-1 Nonimmigrant Students With STEM Degree, it definitely contributes more for American society. Nowadays, there are a large amount of international students coming to USA to pursue a higher degree in STEM. If they have to go back because of visa issue after graduation, they will only work and contributes what they have learned in USA to their own country. But if they are given the opportunity to work in USA after graduation, they will be able to use what they learned in USA. Especially, people with Master or PhD degree can contribute more to the R&D department of companies.
