This proposal is beneficial to both the academia and the industry in science and technology. Foreign students in STEM majors are making substantial contributions to this country as the workforce of native people in science and technology industry has always been insufficient for the rapid development in this globalization era. The new proposal provides a good opportunity for this country to retain talents from foreign countries in academia and industry. It also makes sure the higher education system can continuously appeals to talent students globally as the higher education opportunities in Australia and Europe are becoming more and more popular. Applaud for this new proposal as it is the wise decision to ensure the competitiveness of the United States in the future.
