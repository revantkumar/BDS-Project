Zenia Z The value of the melting pot lies in its diversity and freedom. Each year, thousands and millions of nonimmigrant international students come to the U.S.,not only for their own personal development, but also to bring in new strength for the States. Students are attracted by the opportunities, bringing with them their dreams and coming to expand their knowledge and horizon, with a sincere wish to gain an opportunity to gain some hands-on experience after graduation. The Americans, in turn, benefit from this fresh and young labor, and open up a great platform for searching new talents for the country and for their own as all. The STEM is an area that worth putting efforts to provide young talents with a platform, to practice for themselves and serve for others in the long term. There is no doubt that this is a win-win situation for all.
