I support the OPT extension without reservation.

President Obama's executive directives to reform the immigration system is not the signs of wanton brinkmanship and runaway autocracy. Rather, they are the corollary of Republican stonewalling and ultra-obstructionism.

For the US to regain, or retain, relevance to global economy and technological competency, we must attract and retain talents from the world over. Many companies in the bay area, where I'm running a cloud computing service, find American grads' qualifications and competencies pale in comparison with those of international students from Japan, China, etc..., who are much more hard-working and law-abiding than any others with whom I have had the opportunity to work.
