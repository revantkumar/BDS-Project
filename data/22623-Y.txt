The opt provides international students a good opportunity to get employment in United States. During the opt period, the student can learn better how they fit in the job market. It is also a good opportunity for the industry to employ these newly graduated students, and make use of their talent. It is quite obvious how much the industry, especially the high tech companies, benefit from these talents. These companies will also get 2 or 3 years to see how these students fit in their position. And they also get the chance to hire someone who gets 2 or 3 year experience during his or her opt. Maintaining the opt STEM extension and extending it to 24 month will definitely benefit the entire industry. It will promote the competitiveness of US-based companies and, finally, benefit US.
