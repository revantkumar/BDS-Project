The OPT Extension program can ensure the United States dominating the technology industry across the globe by providing talented engineering and science students an opportunity to stay longer. International students are always smart to get an offer from an US college. And just imagine, if this OPT program got canceled, where would these smart students go for career and make contribution? Plus, if the opportunity got dismissed, could the tech industry afford losing so many employees all of a sudden? To avoid these two hypotheses, approving OPT extension program could be a key.
