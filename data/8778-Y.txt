OPT gives training opportunities for F-1 Non immigrant students with STEM Degrees. STEM student's research help in development of new technologies and advancement in existing technologies. Which in turn generates new work and revenue in the exiting or new businesses helping American job seeker to get jobs every year. Because any other full time work visa depends on lottery system there is no guarantee that F-1 students will get full time work visa in given amount of time. The only way F-1 students can increase their chances of getting full time work visas is that they are getting more period in OPT.
