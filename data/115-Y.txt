The greatness of this country lies in its open-mindedness. "The land of the free and the home of the brave" allows all talents compete equally to contribute to the prospering of the country. Surely American citizens work hard to get a STEM job but the fact is that OPT students have to work harder to get to a similar position. The 24-month OPT extension shows the nation's willingness to welcome true talents from the globe and determination to keep moving forward.
