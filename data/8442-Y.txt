Sudeep Panigrahy, CSULB The new intended amendment to the opt period would be undoubtedly beneficial not only for the students but also for this great country. The primary reason for F1 students coming here apart from the meticulous education is the pragmatic exposure in their related fields, industrially speaking. And the extended opt duration would help them develop themselves before they jump into their vocations which would allow them to work with utmost efficiency, thus also contributing to the prosperity of the USA.
