Lenore Swanberg The Optional Practical Training (OPT) program, which gives employers financial incentives to hire foreign students and recent college graduates over Americans.
This would put downward pressure on wages for all tech workers. Furthermore, the program actually gives incentives to employers to hire foreign students because they don't have to pay the employer portion of payroll taxes or a prevailing wage. Immigration policy should serve in the interests of American workers, not against it. The American people pay taxes and wages for representatives for the future of all of our children. In return for all this money big businesses pay lobbyists and reps to take jobs away from our youth.
