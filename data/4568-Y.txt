One of the most important characteristics of a booming city or country is the fluidity of resource, capital and talent. International students serve as the most valuable source of talents from all over the world. These talents bring their unique expertise and creativity to help America stay competitive globally. America is one of the biggest economy entity in the world. It has the demand and capability to absorb many many new people, especially highly talented ones. The international students help create new opportunities and positions which are not previously available. It is already a part of American economy. It is difficult to imagine if we lose these talents.
