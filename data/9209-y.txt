International students also help promote a global learning environment. These benefits will encourage future international students to join STEM programs in the U.S. and allow U.S. companies to remain competitive globally. STEM students will allow employers the opportunity to accomplish bigger projects, achieve more innovation, and develop new products and technology, all of which will further fuel the U.S. economy.
