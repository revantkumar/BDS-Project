This regulation should be applied because the target of it is the international students group, most of them in the age between 20-30 and are highly educated(at least undergraduate), just beginning their careers.

As a country formed by immigrants, it is important it remains opening to all the talents from the world. There are only a small amount of these students will finally stay, since they need a working VISA and GC later. Those who stayed generally work hard and made a lot of contribution to the country. a 24 months STEM will give students more time for training so that these students will be more likely to stay.

Furthermore, It is also beneficial for employers as they have more time to decide whether to file a H1B for students. If they hire someone with only 12 months OPT they don't have enough time to evaluate the student's performance and make decision to file the H1B or not.
